Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Jared Peno ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you visit our websites, purchase our products, or subscribe to our newsletter.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name and email address when you subscribe to our newsletter or create an account
  • Payment information when you make a purchase (processed securely by Stripe — we do not store your card details)
  • Communications you send to us

We also collect certain information automatically when you visit our websites, including IP address, browser type, pages visited, and referring URLs, via standard server logs and analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfill your purchases
  • To deliver products, access links, and support
  • To send you our newsletter and product updates (you can unsubscribe at any time)
  • To respond to your questions and support requests
  • To improve our products and website

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We share it only with trusted service providers who help us operate our business:

  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Beehiiv — newsletter and email delivery
  • Google — product document delivery and analytics
  • Calendly — session scheduling

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Email Communications

When you purchase a product or subscribe to our newsletter, you'll receive emails from us. Every email includes an unsubscribe link. You can also opt out at any time by emailing jaredpeno@simplifyit.co.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. If you'd like us to delete your data, email us and we'll respond within 30 days.

6. Cookies

Our websites may use cookies for analytics and to remember your preferences. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not function properly.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at jaredpeno@simplifyit.co.

8. Children's Privacy

Our products are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll post the updated version on this page with a revised date. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email jaredpeno@simplifyit.co.
